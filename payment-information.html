❓ FAQs

Common Questions

When will I receive my first payout?+
After you complete your payment setup and deliver your first approved lessons, you'll be included in the next Friday payout run. Banks may take 1–2 business days to settle.
What happens with no‑shows or late cancellations?+
Follow the scheduling and no‑show policy. Approved compensations/charges are reflected in the payout for that period.
Can I be paid in USD?+
NGN bank transfer is standard. If you require USD, contact Support to discuss feasibility, fees, and timelines.
