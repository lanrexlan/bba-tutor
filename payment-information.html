Payments & Earnings

Payment Information

Understand your earnings, payout schedule, and how to get paid securely. Use the calculator to estimate your take‑home.

Tutor Share

Tutors receive 75% of lesson fees. The platform share covers operations, support, and payment processing.

Payout Window

Payouts are processed weekly on Fridays by 6PM WAT for approved lessons delivered Mon–Sun of the prior week.

Methods

Bank transfer (NGN). For USD or other currencies, contact Support to discuss options and fees.

Taxes

Tutors are independent contractors. Keep your records and comply with local tax laws. We provide payout summaries.

From Lesson to Payout

Track the typical flow. Timings can vary on public holidays or bank delays.

1) Lesson Delivered

You teach a lesson and mark it complete.

2) Approval

Attendance and delivery confirmed; issues flagged if any.

3) Weekly Close (Fri 6PM WAT)

Lessons from the week are batched for payout.

4) Payout Initiated

Transfer submitted to your bank or processor.

5) Funds Received

Bank processing takes ~1–2 business days.
