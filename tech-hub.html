Troubleshooting & Support

Quick fixes for common issues, and a tool to instantly report bugs to our tech team.

  • Close other apps using camera/mic (e.g., Skype, Teams).
  • Check browser address bar settings to allow camera/mic.
  • Unplug and replug USB headsets; try a different port.
  • Always use a headset; mute laptop speakers to prevent feedback loops.
  • Ask students to mute their mics when not speaking.
  • Close unused browser tabs and memory-heavy applications.
  • Disable virtual backgrounds (they require heavy CPU usage).
  • If internet is slow, lower video resolution or switch to audio-only.
