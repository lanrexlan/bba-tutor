Troubleshooting Playbook

Quick fixes to resolve the most common issues during lessons.

  • Close other apps using camera/mic (e.g., other meeting tabs).
  • Check browser site permissions: allow camera and microphone.
  • Select the correct device in meeting settings.
  • Unplug and replug USB devices; try a different USB port.
  • Restart browser; if needed, restart computer.
  • Use a headset; mute laptop speakers to prevent feedback.
  • In the platform, enable noise suppression if available.
  • Ask students to mute when not speaking; avoid dual devices nearby.
  • Check input level and device selected.
  • Move closer to router; switch to 5 GHz if supported.
  • Turn off HD video; disable incoming video when needed.
  • Close downloads/streaming; pause cloud backups.
  • Use a wired connection or mobile hotspot as backup.
  • Close unused tabs/apps; clear temporary files.
  • Disable virtual background; use static image or plain wall.
  • Lower video resolution to 360p or audio‑only as needed.
  • Keep your browser up to date.
  • Share a specific window/tab to reduce bandwidth.
  • Enable “Share system audio” only when needed.
  • Close sensitive content; tidy your desktop before sharing.
