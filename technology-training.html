🛠️ Playbook

Troubleshooting Playbook

Quick fixes for the most common issues during lessons.

📹 Camera or Mic Not Working

  1. Close other apps using camera/mic.
  2. Check browser site permissions: allow camera and microphone.
  3. Select the correct device in meeting settings.
  4. Unplug and replug USB devices; try a different port.
  5. Restart browser; if needed, restart computer.

🔊 Students Can't Hear Me / Echo

  1. Use a headset; mute laptop speakers to prevent feedback.
  2. Enable noise suppression if available.
  3. Ask students to mute when not speaking.
  4. Check input level and device selected.

📶 Weak Internet / Video Freezing

  1. Move closer to router; switch to 5 GHz if supported.
  2. Turn off HD video; disable incoming video when needed.
  3. Close downloads/streaming; pause cloud backups.
  4. Use a wired connection or mobile hotspot as backup.

🐢 Low Computer Performance

  1. Close unused tabs/apps; clear temporary files.
  2. Disable virtual background; use static image.
  3. Lower video resolution to 360p or audio‑only.
  4. Keep your browser up to date.
