🛡️ Standards & Safety

Platform Guidelines

Our shared standards for respectful, safe, and high‑quality teaching. Review carefully and acknowledge to start tutoring.

📋 Code of Conduct

  • Be punctual, prepared, and professional in all communications.
  • Use respectful, inclusive language; honour names and pronouns.
  • Avoid conflicts of interest; keep tutor–student boundaries clear.
  • Do not share personal contact details outside the platform.

🛡️ Safeguarding & Child Protection

  • Never meet students in private channels; keep communication on approved platforms.
  • Report any safeguarding concern immediately to Support.
  • Use age‑appropriate materials; avoid sensitive or graphic content.
  • No recording or sharing of student images without explicit guardian consent.

🔒 Data Privacy & Security

  • Protect student data; do not download/share outside approved systems.
  • Use strong passwords and enable device lock screens.
  • Only store lesson files in approved cloud folders with correct permissions.

📝 Academic Integrity & Assessment

  • Support learning without doing work for students.
  • Use approved rubrics and give timely, actionable feedback.
  • Moderate assessments with colleagues for fairness.

📅 Scheduling, Attendance & Cancellations

  • Confirm time zones; start and end on time.
  • Follow no‑show policy (wait at least 10 minutes; document).
  • For changes within 24 hours, secure guardian approval and update the scheduler.

💬 Communications & Professional Boundaries

  • Keep communications on the platform; keep tone warm and professional.
  • Avoid personal social media connections with students.
  • Escalate difficult conversations to Support promptly.

🎥 Recording & Consent

  • Record only if required and with explicit consent; notify participants at the start.
  • Store recordings securely and follow retention timelines.
⚖️ Enforcement

Consequences & Strikes Policy

1

Reminder/Coaching

2

Formal Warning

If issues persist or are more serious.

3

Final Warning/Suspension

For repeated or significant breaches.

4

Termination

For severe violations (e.g., safeguarding breach).
