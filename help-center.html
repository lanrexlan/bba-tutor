Common Questions

What if a student doesn't join the session within 10 minutes?+
Wait at least 10 minutes while messaging the guardian. Document the no-show in the platform and offer a brief recap resource. Follow the Payment Information policy for no‑shows.
Can I reschedule a lesson within 24 hours?+
Yes, if the guardian agrees. Use the scheduling tool to propose 2–3 slots. Reconfirm time zone and update calendar invites.
How do I handle sensitive topics in class?+
Follow Platform Guidelines and Safeguarding. Keep discussion factual, inclusive, and age‑appropriate. Escalate concerns to support.
