Curriculum Expectations

Explore the UK National Curriculum core strands and assessment types for your subject area.

Science (Biology, Chemistry, Physics)

Recommended: 180–240 mins/week | Target: UK GCSE Standard

Core Strands

  • Scientific Inquiry & Data Analysis
  • Life Sciences (Biology)
  • Matter & Reactions (Chemistry)
  • Forces & Energy (Physics)

Assessment Types

  • Virtual Lab Reports
  • Data Interpretation Tasks
  • Weekly Mini-Quizzes
  • End-of-Unit GCSE Style Exams

Mathematics

Recommended: 180–240 mins/week | Target: UK GCSE Standard

Core Strands

  • Number & Operations
  • Algebraic Thinking
  • Geometry & Measurement
  • Data & Probability

Assessment Types

  • Weekly Problem Sets
  • Real-world application tasks
  • Mental Math checks
  • End-of-Unit GCSE Style Exams

English Language & Literature

Recommended: 240–300 mins/week | Target: UK GCSE Standard

Core Strands

  • Reading Comprehension
  • Creative & Academic Writing
  • Speaking & Listening
  • Grammar & Vocabulary

Assessment Types

  • Analytical Essays
  • Creative Writing Portfolios
  • Oral Presentations
  • Reading Logs
